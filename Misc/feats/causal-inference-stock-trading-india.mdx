### **From Correlation to Causation**

Markets don’t move randomly. Earnings, sentiment, policy, and global cues all interact.\
Causal inference helps separate _noise_ from _reason_.

**Why it matters in India:**

- Policy shocks and budget cycles drive volatility.
- Retail inflows can distort momentum.
- Corporate events change narratives overnight.

OpenKuber translates this complexity into readable signals right inside Sheets—helping you trade on **reasons, not reactions**.
