### **Step-by-Step**

1. **Install OpenKuber.**
2. **Fetch live tickers (NSE/BSE).**
3. **Add portfolio tables & alerts.**
4. **Use formulas for reasoning (e.g., WHYPRICE()).**
5. **Style with conditional formatting & charts.**
6. **Share with clients or teams.**

Result: A custom dashboard tracking your positions, market events, and reasoning in real time.
